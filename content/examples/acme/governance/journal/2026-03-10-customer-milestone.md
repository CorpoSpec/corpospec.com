# 100th Customer Milestone

**Date:** 2026-03-10

## Context

Hit 100 paying customers today. Took 9 months from launch. Growth accelerating: first 50 took 6 months, second 50 took 3 months.

## Observations

1. Conversion rate from free to paid improved from 3% to 5.2% after the onboarding flow redesign (BDR 0025). The guided setup wizard with pre-built templates made the difference.

2. Average contract value is climbing. Started at $180/month, now at $310/month. The AI workflow templates command premium pricing. Customers who use AI templates have 3x higher retention.

3. Churn dropped to 2.1%. The biggest churn risk is companies that sign up but never complete onboarding. The 72-hour follow-up email sequence reduced this by 40%.

4. Top customer concentration is healthy. Largest customer is 4.8% of MRR. No single customer dependency.

## Implications

- The PLG flywheel is working. Self-serve acquisition accounts for 68% of new customers.
- Enterprise tier will accelerate ACV growth. Two pilot customers want to pay $2K+/month.
- Need to hire a dedicated customer success person before 200 customers.
