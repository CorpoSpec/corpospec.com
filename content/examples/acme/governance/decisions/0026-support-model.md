# Tiered Support Model

## Problem Statement

As customer count grows, support volume increases. Acme must define support tiers that balance customer satisfaction with operational efficiency.

## Considered Options

Multiple approaches were evaluated before reaching this decision.

## Decision

Three support tiers: (1) Free tier: community forum and documentation only. (2) Starter/Pro: email support with 24-hour SLA. (3) Enterprise: dedicated Slack channel with 4-hour SLA and named support contact.

## Reasoning

Tiered support aligns service levels with customer value. Free tier customers get self-serve resources. Paid customers get email support. Enterprise customers get the high-touch support they need to justify their investment. This model scales without requiring a large support team at the current stage.
