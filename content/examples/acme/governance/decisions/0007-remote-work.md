# Remote-First with Quarterly Offsites

## Problem Statement

Decide whether Acme operates as remote-first, hybrid, or office-based. This affects hiring geography, overhead costs, and team culture.

## Considered Options

Multiple approaches were evaluated before reaching this decision.

## Decision

Remote-first with quarterly offsites. All team members work remotely. The company hosts 4 offsites per year (3 days each) for strategy, team building, and collaboration. Budget $5,000 per person per offsite.

## Reasoning

Remote-first expands the hiring pool beyond San Francisco. Eliminates office lease costs at a stage where capital efficiency is critical. Quarterly offsites provide sufficient in-person time for relationship building and strategic alignment. Async communication practices (Linear for tasks, Notion for docs, Slack for chat) enable effective remote collaboration.
