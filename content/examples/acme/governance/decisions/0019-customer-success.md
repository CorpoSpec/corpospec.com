# Dedicated CS Team at 50 Customers

## Problem Statement

Customer churn increases without proactive support. The founders currently handle customer success, but this does not scale. Acme must decide when to hire dedicated customer success.

## Considered Options

Multiple approaches were evaluated before reaching this decision.

## Decision

Hire the first Customer Success Manager when paying customer count reaches 50. Target hire before 75 customers. CS team scales at a ratio of 1 CSM per 75 customers.

## Reasoning

At 50 customers, the founders can no longer provide the level of proactive support needed to maintain low churn. A dedicated CSM improves retention through onboarding assistance, regular check-ins, and expansion opportunities. The 1:75 ratio balances cost efficiency with service quality.
