# SMB-First Market Strategy

## Problem Statement

Acme must decide whether to target SMBs, mid-market, or enterprise customers first. This decision affects product features, pricing, and go-to-market approach.

## Considered Options

Multiple approaches were evaluated before reaching this decision.

## Decision

Target SMBs first (10-200 employees). Build self-serve onboarding and product-led growth mechanics. Expand to mid-market after achieving PMF with SMBs.

## Reasoning

SMBs have shorter sales cycles (days vs months), lower compliance requirements, and provide faster feedback loops for product iteration. PLG mechanics work best with SMBs. The mid-market and enterprise expansion follows once the product has proven value at scale. Starting enterprise-first would require a sales team and compliance certifications before generating meaningful revenue.
