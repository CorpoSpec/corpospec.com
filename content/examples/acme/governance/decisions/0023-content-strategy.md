# Developer-Focused Content Strategy

## Problem Statement

Acme needs organic traffic and brand awareness. Content marketing is the primary scalable channel for a PLG company at this stage.

## Considered Options

Multiple approaches were evaluated before reaching this decision.

## Decision

Produce developer-focused content: technical tutorials, workflow automation guides, API documentation, and case studies. Publish 3 blog posts per week. Maintain a YouTube channel with bi-weekly tutorial videos.

## Reasoning

Developer-focused content attracts technical evaluators who champion tool adoption within their organizations. SEO-optimized tutorials generate compounding organic traffic. Case studies provide social proof for purchase decisions. The $5K monthly budget covers a freelance technical writer and video editing.
