# 15% ESOP Reservation

## Problem Statement

Acme needs an employee stock option pool to attract and retain talent. The pool size affects founder dilution and hiring competitiveness.

## Considered Options

Multiple approaches were evaluated before reaching this decision.

## Decision

Reserve 15% of fully diluted shares for the employee stock option pool (ESOP). Standard 4-year vesting with 1-year cliff. Board approval required for individual grants.

## Reasoning

15% is standard for seed-stage SaaS companies. It provides sufficient equity for 15-20 early employees at competitive grant sizes. The pool is pre-allocated before Series A pricing, minimizing dilution to Series A investors.
