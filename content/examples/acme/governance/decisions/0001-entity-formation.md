# C-Corp Formation in Delaware

## Problem Statement

Acme needs a legal entity to accept customers, hire employees, and raise venture capital. Jurisdiction and entity type affect fundraising, tax treatment, and operational flexibility.

## Considered Options

Multiple approaches were evaluated before reaching this decision.

## Decision

Form a Delaware C-Corp. Delaware is the default jurisdiction for venture-backed startups. C-Corp structure aligns with standard fundraising instruments (SAFE, priced rounds) and investor expectations.

## Reasoning

Delaware C-Corp eliminates the need for future restructuring. Standard YC SAFE and NVCA documents assume this structure. Formation cost is minimal (~$2,500 including legal fees). Both founders can operate as officers regardless of residence.
