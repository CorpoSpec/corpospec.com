# Product-Led Growth

## Problem Statement

Choose between product-led growth (PLG), sales-led growth (SLG), or a hybrid approach for initial go-to-market.

## Considered Options

Multiple approaches were evaluated before reaching this decision.

## Decision

Product-led growth. Free tier drives top-of-funnel. Self-serve upgrade to paid tiers. Founders handle high-touch sales for enterprise pilots until dedicated sales hire at $25K+ MRR.

## Reasoning

PLG reduces customer acquisition cost and scales without proportional sales headcount. The free tier eliminates adoption friction. Self-serve upgrade captures customers who want to move fast without sales conversations. The founders can handle the small number of enterprise deals in the near term.
