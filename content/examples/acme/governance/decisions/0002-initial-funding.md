# $2.5M Seed Round Strategy

## Problem Statement

Acme needs capital to fund product development, hiring, and initial go-to-market. The company must decide on round size, instrument type, and investor targeting.

## Considered Options

Multiple approaches were evaluated before reaching this decision.

## Decision

Raise a $2.5M seed round via post-money SAFE with a $15M cap. Target a mix of institutional seed funds and strategic angels with SaaS operating experience.

## Reasoning

$2.5M provides 24+ months of runway at the projected burn rate. Post-money SAFE simplifies dilution calculations. $15M cap reflects comparable seed-stage SaaS companies at a similar stage. Institutional lead provides signal for follow-on fundraising.
