# Brand Guidelines v1

## Problem Statement

Acme needs a consistent brand identity across all touchpoints: website, product, documentation, and marketing materials.

## Considered Options

Multiple approaches were evaluated before reaching this decision.

## Decision

Establish brand guidelines v1 with Deep Blue as the primary color, Inter and Plus Jakarta Sans as typefaces, and a "pragmatic enabler" brand personality. The Creator archetype positions Acme as a tool that empowers teams to build.

## Reasoning

A consistent brand identity builds trust and recognition. The Deep Blue palette conveys reliability and professionalism. Inter provides excellent readability for product UI and documentation. The Creator archetype aligns with the product's value proposition of empowering teams to automate and build.
