# GDPR Compliance Plan

## Problem Statement

EU customers and enterprise prospects with EU operations require GDPR compliance. Acme processes personal data (names, emails, workflow data) that falls under GDPR scope.

## Considered Options

Multiple approaches were evaluated before reaching this decision.

## Decision

Implement GDPR compliance in three phases: (1) Privacy policy, cookie consent, and DPA templates by Q2 2026. (2) Data subject access request automation and data deletion workflows by Q3 2026. (3) EU data center deployment and data residency guarantees by Q4 2026.

## Reasoning

GDPR compliance is mandatory for EU market entry (BDR 0020). Phased implementation spreads the cost and effort. Privacy policy and DPAs are table stakes for any enterprise deal. Automated DSAR handling reduces operational burden. EU data residency is the final requirement for enterprise customers with strict data sovereignty policies.
