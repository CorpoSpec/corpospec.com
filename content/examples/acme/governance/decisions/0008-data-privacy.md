# SOC 2 Type II Compliance Path

## Problem Statement

Enterprise customers require security certifications before purchasing. Acme must decide which compliance frameworks to pursue and in what order.

## Considered Options

Multiple approaches were evaluated before reaching this decision.

## Decision

Pursue SOC 2 Type II as the primary compliance certification. Begin readiness assessment in Q2 2026. Target audit completion by Q4 2026. GDPR compliance in parallel for EU market entry.

## Reasoning

SOC 2 Type II is the most commonly requested certification by US enterprise buyers. It covers security, availability, processing integrity, confidentiality, and privacy. Starting with SOC 2 creates a foundation that accelerates ISO 27001 and GDPR compliance. The estimated cost of $50K annually includes audit fees, tooling, and ongoing compliance maintenance.
