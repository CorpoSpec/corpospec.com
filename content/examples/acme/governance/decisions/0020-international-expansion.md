# EU Expansion in 2027

## Problem Statement

Several enterprise prospects and existing customers have EU operations requiring data residency. Acme must decide when and how to expand into the EU market.

## Considered Options

Multiple approaches were evaluated before reaching this decision.

## Decision

Proposed: Target EU market entry in Q1 2027. Requires full GDPR compliance (BDR 0027), EU data center deployment, and localized support. Establish an EU subsidiary for contractual purposes.

## Reasoning

The EU market represents a significant portion of the SAM ($4.3B). However, premature expansion diverts resources from US market dominance. Targeting Q1 2027 provides time to achieve SOC 2 and GDPR compliance, establish EU infrastructure, and build the support team needed for a multi-timezone operation.
