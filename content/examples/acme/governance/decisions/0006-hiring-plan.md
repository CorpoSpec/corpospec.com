# Engineering-First Hiring Plan

## Problem Statement

With $2.5M in seed capital, Acme must decide hiring priorities and sequencing. Engineering, sales, marketing, and customer success all need headcount.

## Considered Options

Multiple approaches were evaluated before reaching this decision.

## Decision

Hire engineering first. Target 8 engineers by end of Q1 2026 before hiring any go-to-market roles. Sales and customer success hiring begins when MRR exceeds $15K.

## Reasoning

Product-led growth requires a strong product before investing in sales. Engineering headcount directly accelerates feature development and reduces time-to-market. At the current stage, the founders handle sales and customer success. Premature go-to-market hiring burns capital on a product that is not yet differentiated enough.
