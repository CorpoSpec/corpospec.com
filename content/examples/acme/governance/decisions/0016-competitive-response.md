# Focus on Product Differentiation

## Problem Statement

Large incumbents (monday.com, Asana, ClickUp) are adding AI-powered workflow features. Acme must decide how to respond to competitive pressure.

## Considered Options

Multiple approaches were evaluated before reaching this decision.

## Decision

Focus on product differentiation rather than competitive marketing. The AI workflow templates that learn from usage patterns are the moat. Invest engineering resources in deepening AI capabilities rather than matching feature lists.

## Reasoning

Competing on features against well-funded incumbents is a losing strategy. Differentiation through AI that learns and adapts creates defensibility that static rule-based automation cannot replicate. Customers choose Acme for intelligent automation, not for checkbox feature parity.
