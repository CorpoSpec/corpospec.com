# Monthly Investor Updates

## Problem Statement

Determine the cadence and format of investor communications. Regular updates maintain investor confidence and keep the company top-of-mind for follow-on rounds.

## Considered Options

Multiple approaches were evaluated before reaching this decision.

## Decision

Send monthly investor updates on the first business day of each month. Format includes: key metrics (MRR, burn, runway, customers), product highlights, team updates, asks (introductions, advice), and a forward-looking paragraph.

## Reasoning

Monthly cadence balances transparency with operational overhead. Consistent reporting builds trust with existing investors and creates a track record for Series A conversations. The structured format ensures key information is communicated without requiring extensive preparation time.
